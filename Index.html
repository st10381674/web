Our Services

Comprehensive Industrial Solutions Backed by Expertise

At MEICS, we deliver multi-disciplined industrial services that prioritize safety, quality, and precision. From structural steelworks to mechanical, electrical, and piping installations, our end-to-end solutions empower large-scale projects across various industrial sectors.

Structural Steel

Expert fabrication and erection of structural steel, backed by detailed rigging studies, risk assessments, and corrosion protection to ensure safe and compliant installations.

Learn More
Mechanical Installations

Turnkey installation of industrial equipment including pumps, gearboxes, mills, kilns, and more—ensuring functionality, safety, and compliance across sectors.

Learn More
Electrical Installations

Installation of cable trays, switchgear, transformers, instrumentation, and final terminations—delivering safe and tested electrical systems ready for commissioning.

Learn More
Piping Fabrication & Installation

High-standard piping services across material types, with certified welding processes and non-destructive testing to ensure durability and zero-defect delivery.

Learn More
HSE & Quality Management

Robust systems for Health, Safety, Environment, and Quality Control that protect people, preserve the environment, and guarantee top-tier project outcomes.

Project Planning & Delivery
